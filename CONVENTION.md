# CONVENTION FOR AMENDMENT
(Quy ước sửa Luật – Hệ Uyên / Trung Nguyên)

This convention governs **how** and **when** the Law (LAW.md) may be amended.

## I. Hierarchy
1. OATH.md is immutable.
2. LAW.md is binding under OATH.md.
3. This convention applies only after OATH.md and LAW.md.

## II. Principle
- The Law may be **clarified**, but not **contradicted**.
- No amendment may violate the Oath.
- No amendment may change the core meaning of the Law.

## III. Amendment Rules
An amendment is valid only if:
1. It is recorded in CHANGELOG.md
2. It preserves the original intent
3. It is explicitly marked as an amendment
4. It does not retroactively alter past records

## IV. Authority
- No single derivative work (website, video, channel, document)
  has authority to amend the Law.
- Amendments exist only within this root repository.

## V. Final Clause
If an amendment conflicts with OATH.md,
the amendment is void.

— He Uyên
